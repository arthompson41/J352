Navient compliants spike during pandemic in Illinois, Texas

By Ava Thompson

One of the nation's largest federal student loan servicers,Navient,faced a spike in compliants during the pandemic in Illinois and Texas, according to the Consumer Compliant Database.

Navient has been involved with a growing number of lawsuits, a studentloanplanner.com article reported. More than 66,000 Navient private student loan borrowers were taken advantage of and will recieve some compensation. Some Illinois and Texas Navient borrowers has problems lowering their monthly payment, couldn't temporarily delay payments, or couldn't get flexible paying options for their loan
